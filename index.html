Main Ceremony

1:00 PM 4:00 PM

At Baras Church, half an hour before the scheduled time is a good idea to ensure that all members of the entourage are present and prepared for the event. This buffer allows for any unexpected delays & for smoother transitions and a more relaxed atmosphere for all involved.
